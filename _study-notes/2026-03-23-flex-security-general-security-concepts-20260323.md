## Key Takeaways

Designing a work environment that reduces physical vulnerabilities helps mitigate risk. This is why physical controls are a crucial aspect of the layered approach (Defence in depth). 

Physical controls can include: 

AAA: Authentication, Authorisation, and Accounting. 
Perimeter security, including walls, fences, doors, gates, lockable windows, and more. 
Security guards and dogs. 
Detection systems, including CCTV and ID badges. 
Lighting. 

All these controls demonstrate best practice physical security, but there are lots of other controls available for consideration. At the same time, it’s important to stay on top of emerging threats, that could jeopardise your operations.
